# GovTruth 🇮🇳

## India's Open Government Accountability Platform

> "Sunlight is the best disinfectant" — Louis Brandeis

GovTruth is an independent, non-partisan platform that 
makes Indian government data accessible, verifiable, 
and actionable for every citizen.

---

## What We Do

We take **official government data** and make it 
actually useful:

- 📊 **MP Asset Tracker** — Official ECI affidavit 
  analysis for all 543 MPs across elections
- 🛰️ **Satellite Verification** — Cross-check 
  infrastructure claims with satellite imagery  
- 📋 **CAG Finding Tracker** — Every documented 
  government irregularity and its follow-up status
- 💰 **Contract Watch** — Government tender analysis 
  and political connection mapping
- 🗳️ **Electoral Bond Map** — Complete visualization 
  of disclosed bond data

---

## Our Principles

✅ **Every claim cites official sources**
✅ **No political affiliation — all parties covered equally**  
✅ **No accusations — only data and documented questions**
✅ **Open source — anyone can verify our methodology**
✅ **Citizen funded — no corporate or political funding**

---

## Data Sources

All data comes exclusively from:
- Election Commission of India (eci.gov.in)
- Comptroller & Auditor General (cag.gov.in)
- Parliament of India (sansad.in)
- Ministry of Corporate Affairs (mca.gov.in)
- Central Public Procurement Portal (eprocure.gov.in)
- Public Financial Management System (pfms.nic.in)
- European Space Agency Sentinel-2 Satellite Data

---

## Tech Stack

- **Backend**: Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL
- **Cloud**: AWS (Multi-region for resilience)
- **Satellite**: Google Earth Engine + ESA Sentinel
- **Blockchain**: Immutable record of all findings
- **Frontend**: React.js
